Word Guess

Word Guess Game - Chemistry Edition! The goal of the game is to guess the correct letters to uncover the hidden word. There are 118 elements [as of May 2019] and the game will randomly choose one for the hidden word. You only have 12 incorrect tries and once you run out, you lose! The game will reveal the element and then reset with a new random word and you may play again.
